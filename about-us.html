About Us

Nx Gen Inventors (NGI) is a leading innovator in engineering, software, and manufacturing, with headquarters in Bangalore and operations in Krishnagiri. Renowned for delivering transformative solutions, NGI integrates precision engineering, advanced software development, and cutting-edge manufacturing to serve various industries.
