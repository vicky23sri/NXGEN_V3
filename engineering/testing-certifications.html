Our testing & certifications

Regulatory Compliance Testing

Ensures products meet industry standards and legal requirements.

  • Guarantee safety, quality, and adherence to regulations.
  • Facilitate market access and consumer trust through certified compliance.

Environmental Testing

Environmental Testing ensures that products can withstand various environmental conditions, and compliance with regulatory standards.

  • Assesses product performance under conditions like temperature, and shock.
  • Supports certification processes by confirming products meet industry and environmental requirements.

EMC & EMI Testing

Testing to ensure devices operate without causing or being affected by electromagnetic interference.

  • Ensures device compatibility with other electronic equipment.
  • Confirms compliance with industry regulations

Product Safety Certification

Verifies that a product meets safety standards to ensure consumer safety.

  • Regulatory Compliance: Confirms adherence to safety regulations.
  • Consumer Trust: Builds confidence in product safety.

Performance & Reliability Testing

Ensures a system performs well and remains dependable.

  • Performance: Measures speed and scalability.
  • Reliability: Assesses consistent functionality.

IP (Ingress Protection) Testing

IP testing assesses the degree of protection provided by enclosures against solid objects and liquids.

  • Rates resistance to dust and foreign objects.
  • Evaluates resistance to water ingress under specified conditions.

Documentation & Certification Assistance

Support in preparing documents for testing and certification.

  • Expert Guidance on compliance.
  • Streamlined Processes for timely approvals.
